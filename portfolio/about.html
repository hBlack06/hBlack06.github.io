About Me

Hi! I’m Holly Black — a Cybersecurity major at High Point University, graduating in December 2025. I’m passionate about technology, problem solving, and innovation in digital security.

My academic and professional experiences have given me hands-on knowledge in areas such as network defense, programming, ethical hacking, and digital forensics. I enjoy working on projects that combine creativity and technical skill to strengthen systems and protect information.

Outside of academics, I value collaboration, leadership, and continual learning. Whether it’s participating in cybersecurity competitions or exploring new hobbies, I’m always looking for ways to grow and contribute to the world.
